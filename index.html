Love Letter

For the one who makes my world soft

From One Letter to Forever I — just One letter, a word that once defined my world. For the longest time, it was only I—my dreams, my days, my little universe. But then you arrived so quietly, yet so beautifully, and somehow that lonely letter became We. It became Us. Out of all the words life has ever rewritten for me, that is the one I treasure the most. My — only Two letters, yet they carry a feeling too profound for words. They are small enough to fit on a page, yet vast enough to hold someone as extraordinary as you. Whenever I say "my love" or "my favorite person," it is your heart that those two letters always find. You — Three letters that always come before the words I love. They are simple, yet they hold the entire reason my heart beats a little faster. Of all the people this world could have placed beside me, I am endlessly grateful that you are the "you" in my I love you. Home — Four letters that once meant nothing more than walls, a roof, and the place where I slept. But loving you changed that meaning forever. Home is no longer where I rest—it is where my heart feels safe. Home is eating beside you, laughing with you, falling asleep knowing you're there, and waking up hoping every tomorrow still has you in it. Home... is you. Peace — Five letters that so many people spend their whole lives searching for. Some chase it through success, others through distant places, and some never find it at all. Yet somehow, I was blessed enough to discover it every time I'm with you. In your presence, the world grows quieter, my worries become lighter, and my heart finally knows what peace truly feels like. JANELA — six letters. Six simple letters that somehow contain a universe I could never fully describe. No dictionary could ever define what your name means to me. Life has always been beautiful, but with you, it became breathtaking. The sunsets seem warmer, painted in deeper shades of gold. The moon shines brighter than it ever did before. Even the gentle breeze feels softer, as though the world itself knows I'm walking through it with you. And now, as we celebrate these beautiful Seven months together, I find myself grateful for the little things that have become my greatest blessings. I found someone who watches sunsets with me instead of letting me watch them alone. Someone who turns ordinary meals into favorite memories. Someone who makes every adventure feel like home, simply because you're there. Thank you for choosing to walk beside me. If love could be measured by words, mine would never be enough. So instead, I'll simply keep choosing you— today, tomorrow, and for every sunset that follows.
